INTRODUCTION

Oyo state is an inland state in southwestern of Nigeria.Its capital is ibadan. oyo state is the most populous city in Nigeria and formaly the second most populous city in africa and nickname "the pace setter state". Cassava, cocoa and tobbaco are among the most important crop of oyo state's economy.

Oyo state consist of 33 local government areas and 29 local government development areas
