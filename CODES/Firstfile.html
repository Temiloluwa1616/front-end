About sun flower

sun flower symbolize loyality and adoration.it is also considered as oilseed. read more!

COLOUR FOR SUNFLOWER

  • Yellow sunflower: it means goodness, truth and timelessness
  • Red sunflower: it means strength and positiviy
  • Orange sunflower: it means energy, happiness and good fortune
  • white sunflower: it means re-birth, peace and purity

USES OF SUNFLOWER

  1. The leaves are used as fodder
  2. The flowers yield a yellow dye
  3. The seed contain oil
